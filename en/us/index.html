MISSION

To provide innovative services and technologies that facilitate inclusive mobility and autonomy for people with reduced mobility (PRM) internationally, promoting respect for their rights and their social inclusion.

VISION

AsoPMR aspires to be a leading organization in promoting inclusive and sustainable mobility for the collective.

VALUES

01

Active Listening,

to the problems of people with reduced mobility to establish a dialogue with institutions and stakeholders that allows these problems to be resolved.

02

Practical Solutions,

innovative and sustainable with a positive and integrating impact on the lives of people that optimize the use of human and environmental resources.

03

Community Focus,

to build relationships and coalitions that facilitate understanding between different groups in society, in order to live together more justly and prosperously.

04

Inspiring Resilience,

improving the ability to relate, adapt, and respond to the challenges that arise throughout life thanks to attitudes, tools, and resources that strengthen society.

05

Global Perspective,

to coordinate international efforts and neutralize the obstacles that prevent free access to events, experiences, and services beyond the immediate environment of people with reduced mobility.
