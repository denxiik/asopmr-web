About Us                        
   
       
           
               

MISSION

               

Our mission is to provide innovative services and technologies that foster inclusive mobility                     and autonomy for people with reduced mobility (PRM) across borders. We aim to promote respect for their                     rights and ensure their social inclusion.

           
           
               

VISION

               
AsoPMR's vision is to be a leading organization in fostering inclusive and sustainable                     mobility for the PRM community.
           
       
       
                               
   
   
       
                       

OUR VALUES

       
       
           
               
                   

01

                   
Active Listening
                   

We listen to the challenges faced by people with reduced mobility to engage in dialogue with institutions and stakeholders, finding effective solutions.

               
               
                   

02

                   
Practical Solutions
                   

We develop innovative and sustainable solutions that have a positive and integrating                         impact, optimizing human and environmental resources.

               
               
                   

03

                   
Community Focus
                   

We build relationships and coalitions to foster understanding among different societal groups, striving for a more just and prosperous coexistence.                    

               
           
           
               
                   

04

                   
Inspiring Resilience
                   

We enhance the ability to adapt and respond to life's challenges through attitudes, tools, and resources that strengthen society.

               
               
                   

05

                   
Global Perspective
                   

We coordinate international efforts to remove barriers, ensuring free access to events, experiences, and services beyond the immediate environment of people with reduced mobility.
