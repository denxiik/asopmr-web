Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are automatically inserted into telematics devices used by the user when visiting a website. These downloaded files can store data that can be updated and retrieved by the entity responsible for their installation. These files allow us to understand, for statistical purposes, service improvement, and adaptation to your preferences, your Browse behavior on the web. Cookies are associated only with your browser and do not provide personal data on their own. The cookies used on this website do not contain viruses or other harmful components, and do not pose a risk to the user's devices by themselves.

This website uses the following cookies:

Types of cookies according to the entity that manages them:

  • First-party cookies: These are cookies set by the website visited by the user. The data collected through these cookies is used for purposes such as calculating page views, sessions, and the number of users.
  • Third-party cookies: These are cookies sent to the user's terminal equipment from a device or domain not managed by the editor, but by another entity that processes data obtained through cookies, or cookies installed from a device or domain managed by the editor itself, but the information collected is managed by a third party.

Types of cookies according to their purpose:

  • Technical/Necessary cookies: These allow the user to perform basic functions such as Browse the page and accessing secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies, so their installation is mandatory.
  • Advertising cookies: These are cookies that, whether processed by this website or by third parties, allow the most effective management possible of the advertising spaces on the website, adapting the content of the advertisement to the content of the requested service or to your use of our website. For this purpose, we can analyze your internet Browse habits and show you advertising related to your Browse profile.
  • Behavioral advertising cookies: These allow for the most effective management possible of the advertising spaces that, where applicable, the editor has included on a web page, application, or platform from which the requested service is provided. This type of cookies stores information on the behavior of visitors obtained through continuous observation of their Browse habits, which allows for the development of a specific profile to show advertising notices based on it.
