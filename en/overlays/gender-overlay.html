AsoPMR GENDER EQUALITY PLAN

1. INTRODUCTION

The Association of People with Reduced Mobility (AsoPMR) is committed to promoting gender equality in all its areas of action, ensuring an inclusive and equitable environment for all people, regardless of their gender. This plan establishes the strategic guidelines to prevent and eliminate any type of gender discrimination within the organization and in its projects.

2. OBJECTIVES

  • Promote equal opportunities in access and professional development within AsoPMR.
  • Integrate a gender perspective into all activities, services, and projects of the association.
  • Prevent discrimination and ensure a respectful and inclusive work environment.
  • Raise awareness and train the team on the importance of gender equality.
  • Promote equitable representation of women and men in decision-making bodies.

3. AREAS OF APPLICATION AND MEASURES

3.1. Access and professional promotion

  • Guarantee internal selection and promotion processes based on objective and transparent criteria.
  • Promote women's access to positions of responsibility and leadership within the organization.
  • Establish measures for reconciling work, personal, and family life.

3.2. Awareness and training

  • Promote gender equality training programs for the team and collaborators.
  • Promote awareness campaigns on discrimination and gender-based violence.
  • Incorporate a gender perspective into all training activities of the entity.

3.3. Organizational culture and inclusive communication

  • Use inclusive and non-sexist language in all AsoPMR communications and documents.
  • Highlight the contribution of women in the field of inclusive mobility and accessibility.
  • Ensure safe spaces for reporting any type of discrimination or harassment.

3.4. Projects and services with a gender perspective

  • Include the gender variable in the design, implementation, and evaluation of projects.
  • Encourage the active participation of women in accessibility and mobility initiatives.
  • Collect gender-disaggregated data to improve equity in the proposed solutions.

4. MONITORING AND EVALUATION

  • Create an equality committee within AsoPMR to monitor the plan.
  • Establish impact indicators to measure the degree of compliance with actions.
  • Conduct periodic evaluations and establish mechanisms for continuous improvement.

5. COMMITMENT AND RESPONSIBILITY

AsoPMR is committed to applying this Gender Equality Plan as part of its social inclusion and sustainability strategy, contributing to a more just and equitable society.
